# Koi unlocks climate investing.

*Last updated: {packageJson.lastUpdated} &bull; Docs Version: {packageJson.version}*

Koi represents a paradigm shift in assessing the impact of technologies on net-zero strategies,
delivering the missing piece for investors, innovators, and industries to act with confidence.

Compared to traditional climate tools, Koi is intuitive, forward-looking, and the first **composable
impact forecasting platform** that can be used by climate innovators, investors, and industries.

1. Start by exploring <a href={`${baseUrl}`} target="_blank">starter forecasts</a> for technologies and markets inside **Koi Studio**.
2. Then scale your decision-making with <a href={`${baseUrl}/forecasts`} target="_blank">customizable</a>, [auditable data](/docs/key-concepts#platform-features) to maximize impact and trust.

Koi provides several options to get started:

- <a href={`${baseUrl}`} target="_blank"><strong>Koi Studio</strong></a>: Explore, forecast, and compare climate technologies with ease.
- [**RESTful API**](/docs/api/reference): Integrate our robust climate forecasting engine into your workflows with a developer-friendly interface.
- [**Koi SDKs**](/docs/api/sdks): Build custom applications with deep integration capabilities.
- [**Documentation**](/docs/quickstart): Comprehensive documentation to guide you every step of the way.
