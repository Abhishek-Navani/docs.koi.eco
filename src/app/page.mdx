# Koi is the world's most powerful forecasting platform to shape impactful climate investments.

Koi represents a paradigm shift in assessing the impact of technologies on net-zero strategies,
delivering the missing piece for investors, innovators, and industries to act with confidence.

Compared to traditional climate tools, Koi is intuitive, forward-looking, and the first **composable
impact forecasting platform** that can be used by climate innovators, investors, and industries.

1. Start by exploring <a href={`${baseUrl}`} target="_blank">starter forecasts</a> for technologies and markets that Koi has already analyzed.
2. Then scale your decision-making with <a href={`${baseUrl}/forecasts`} target="_blank">customizable</a>, [auditable data](/key-concepts#platform-features) to maximize impact and trust.

Koi provides several options to get started:

- <a href={`${baseUrl}`} target="_blank"><strong>Koi Studio</strong></a>: Explore, forecast, and compare climate technologies with ease.
- [**RESTful API**](/rest-api): Integrate our robust climate forecasting engine into your workflows with a developer-friendly interface.
- [**Koi SDKs**](/sdks): Build custom applications with deep integration capabilities.
- [**Documentation**](/quickstart): Comprehensive documentation to guide you every step of the way.
